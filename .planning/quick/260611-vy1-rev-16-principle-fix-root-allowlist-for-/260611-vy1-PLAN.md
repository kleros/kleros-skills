<objective>
Replace the predicate-symmetric ABSENT assertions in the "Sanity check stripped tree" step of `sync-master.yml` with a positive root-file allowlist (REV-16 principle fix).

Purpose: The current check asserts absence using the same predicates as the strip step. When the strip's regex is wrong — exactly what happened with `FEEDBACK_FROM_CLAUDE_MARKETPLACE.md` in SEED-002 Phase 8 — the verifier is blind to the same files the strip missed. The fix expresses the check in terms of the expected OUTCOME (what root looks like after a correct strip), not the operation (what we tried to remove).

Output: `sync-master.yml` with allowlist-based sanity check; one atomic commit.
</objective>
